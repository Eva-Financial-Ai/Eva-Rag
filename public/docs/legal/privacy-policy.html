Privacy Policy

EVA Financial AI Platform

Effective Date: December 1, 2024
Last Updated: December 1, 2024
Important: This Privacy Policy governs the collection, use, and disclosure of personal information by EVA Financial AI in connection with our commercial lending platform and related financial services. Please read this policy carefully before using our services.

1. Introduction

EVA Financial AI ("we," "us," or "our") operates a commercial lending platform that connects borrowers with lenders and provides various financial technology services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and handling your personal information responsibly and in compliance with applicable laws.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, share, and protect your personal information when you use our platform, whether as a borrower, lender, broker, or other user.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Identity Information: Name, date of birth, Social Security Number, government-issued ID numbers
  • Contact Information: Email address, phone numbers, mailing address, business address
  • Financial Information: Bank account details, credit history, income information, tax records, financial statements
  • Business Information: Business name, EIN, business structure, ownership details, industry information
  • Transaction Information: Loan applications, payment history, account activity
  • Communication Preferences: Email, SMS, and phone call preferences

2.2 Information We Collect Automatically

  • Technical Information: IP address, browser type, device information, operating system
  • Usage Information: Pages visited, time spent on platform, click patterns, feature usage
  • Location Information: General geographic location based on IP address
  • Cookies and Tracking: Information collected through cookies, web beacons, and similar technologies

2.3 Information from Third Parties

  • Credit Bureaus: Credit reports, credit scores, credit monitoring information
  • Bank Account Verification: Account ownership verification, transaction history
  • Government Databases: Business registration records, professional licenses
  • Data Providers: Business information, marketing data, fraud prevention data
  • Social Media: Publicly available information from social media platforms

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Primary Business Purposes

  • Processing and evaluating loan applications
  • Conducting credit checks and risk assessments
  • Facilitating lending transactions between borrowers and lenders
  • Providing customer support and account management
  • Processing payments and managing loan servicing

3.2 Operational Purposes

  • Verifying your identity and preventing fraud
  • Monitoring platform security and preventing unauthorized access
  • Analyzing platform performance and user experience
  • Developing and improving our services
  • Maintaining records for regulatory compliance

3.3 Communication Purposes

  • Sending transaction notifications and status updates
  • Providing customer support and responding to inquiries
  • Sending regulatory notices and important account information
  • Marketing our services (with your consent)
  • Conducting surveys and collecting feedback

4. Information Sharing and Disclosure

4.1 With Your Consent

We may share your information with third parties when you have given us explicit consent to do so, including:

  • Sharing loan applications with potential lenders
  • Providing information to service providers you have selected
  • Sharing information for marketing purposes (where permitted)

4.2 Business Partners and Service Providers

  • Lenders: Financial institutions and alternative lenders participating in our platform
  • Credit Bureaus: For credit reporting and monitoring services
  • Banking Partners: For account verification and payment processing
  • Technology Providers: Cloud hosting, data analytics, and security services
  • Professional Services: Legal, accounting, and auditing firms

4.3 Legal and Regulatory Requirements

We may disclose your information when required by law or regulation, including:

  • Responding to subpoenas, court orders, or legal process
  • Complying with banking and financial regulations
  • Reporting to regulatory agencies as required
  • Cooperating with law enforcement investigations
  • Protecting our rights and property

4.4 Business Transactions

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of the business transaction, subject to appropriate confidentiality protections.

5. Data Security

5.1 Security Measures

We implement comprehensive security measures to protect your personal information:

  • Encryption: All sensitive data is encrypted both in transit and at rest using industry-standard encryption
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls and authentication requirements for all personnel
  • Network Security: Firewalls, intrusion detection, and regular security monitoring
  • Compliance: SOC 2 Type II compliance and regular security audits
  • Data Minimization: Collection and retention of only necessary information

5.2 Electronic Signatures

Electronic signatures are secured using:

  • IP address and timestamp logging
  • Geographic location tracking
  • Document integrity verification
  • Tamper-evident audit trails
  • Multi-factor authentication when required

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Correction

You have the right to:

  • Access and review your personal information
  • Request corrections to inaccurate information
  • Update your account information and preferences
  • Download a copy of your data

6.2 Communication Preferences

You can control how we communicate with you:

  • Email preferences: Opt in/out of marketing emails
  • SMS preferences: Opt in/out of text message notifications
  • Phone preferences: Manage auto-dialer consent
  • Marketing preferences: Control promotional communications

6.3 State-Specific Rights

California Residents (CCPA/CPRA)

  • Right to know what personal information is collected
  • Right to delete personal information
  • Right to opt-out of sale of personal information
  • Right to non-discrimination for exercising privacy rights
  • Right to correct inaccurate personal information

European Union Residents (GDPR)

  • Right of access to personal data
  • Right to rectification of inaccurate data
  • Right to erasure ("right to be forgotten")
  • Right to restrict processing
  • Right to data portability
  • Right to object to processing

7. Data Retention

7.1 Retention Periods

Information Type Retention Period Legal Basis
Loan Applications 7 years after completion or denial Federal lending regulations
Credit Reports 7 years Fair Credit Reporting Act
Transaction Records 7 years Banking regulations
Electronic Signatures Life of loan + 7 years E-SIGN Act requirements
Communication Logs 3 years TCPA compliance
Marketing Data Until opt-out Consent-based processing

7.2 Secure Deletion

When retention periods expire, we securely delete or anonymize your information using industry-standard methods to ensure it cannot be recovered or reconstructed.

8. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

8.1 Types of Cookies We Use

  • Essential Cookies: Required for platform functionality and security
  • Performance Cookies: Help us understand how users interact with our platform
  • Functional Cookies: Remember your preferences and settings
  • Marketing Cookies: Used for targeted advertising (with consent)

8.2 Managing Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings. However, disabling certain cookies may limit platform functionality.

9. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for international transfers, including:

  • Standard Contractual Clauses approved by regulatory authorities
  • Adequacy decisions by relevant data protection authorities
  • Certification schemes and codes of conduct
  • Additional technical and organizational security measures

10. Children's Privacy

Our platform is designed for business and commercial lending purposes and is not intended for individuals under 18 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from minors. If we become aware that we have collected information from a minor, we will take steps to delete such information promptly.

11. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices, technology, legal requirements, or other factors. We will notify you of material changes by:

  • Posting the updated policy on our platform
  • Sending email notifications to registered users
  • Providing in-app notifications
  • For material changes, obtaining renewed consent where required

Your continued use of our platform after changes become effective constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
